[h1]Daily Recipe[/h1]

[b]Version: 1.0.0 | Author: Orange Xiaoxing[/b]

[hr][/hr]

[h2]Mod Features[/h2]

Automatically announces daily recommended recipes during daytime. Consuming the recommended foods grants bonus attribute recovery and special buff effects. Supports both vanilla and modded recipes.

[hr][/hr]

[h2]Effects & Configuration[/h2]

[b]Attribute Bonus[/b]
When consuming daily recipes, health, hunger, and sanity restoration effects receive additional bonuses.
Available multipliers: 1.2x / 1.5x / 2.0x / 2.5x / 3.0x

[b]Extra Buffs[/b]
[list]
[*] Speed: Movement speed increased by 30%
[*] Light: Character emits light around them
[*] Lifesteal: Attacks restore 20% of damage dealt as health
[/list]
Duration: 30s / 60s / 120s / 180s / 300s

[b]Configuration Options[/b]
[list]
[*] Daily recipe count: 1-5 recipes (default 3)
[*] Bonus multiplier: 1.2-3.0x (default 1.5x)
[*] Announcement text: Today's Special / Daily Menu / Chef's Choice / Recommended
[*] Extra buff: None / Speed / Light / Lifesteal
[*] Buff duration: 30-300 seconds
[/list]
